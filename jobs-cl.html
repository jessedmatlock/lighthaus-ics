Careers in Compliance

Compliance jobs are for those who enjoy working with regulatory agencies and companies to ensure that the needs of both groups are met. While compliance is required in virtually all industries, some of the more intriguing opportunities are found in Finance, Management Consulting and the exciting arenas of Emerging Payments and Digital Currencies. These disciplines give compliance officers the opportunity to engage in interesting work, learn about the rules and regulations of emerging fields, and build their careers within these areas. According to the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS), the largest employers of compliance officers are government agencies. Federal, state and local government are the three most popular locales for compliance, with corporate management and insurance companies a distant runners-up.

Careers in the Legal Field

ICS works on a diverse range of Legal roles that do and do not require a JD. ICS prides itself on placing not only in-house Counsel, but also Legal support — Paralegals and Legal Assistants for both companies and law firms. Furthermore, we understand the engagement of Contractors/Consultants can be a dependable stopgap in a fast-paced, production-driven environment. ICS can provide temporary staff on a project basis and as interim staff until finding permanent replacements. This includes e-Discovery, Document Review, Legal Remediations and general Business-as-Usual projects, for instance.
