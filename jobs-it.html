Careers in Information Technology

Career opportunities in information technology are expected to grow by 32 percent by 2018, so this is a wise choice if you are pursuing a new career path. Opportunities in information technology management, Web development, IT support, IT infrastructure and more are readily available for qualified candidates. The majority of those with careers in the IT sector have a degree in computer science with an emphasis on math and physics, though practical experience does play a key role in securing an IT position. A background and training in computer science and programming languages such as Java, Microsoft.NET, and C++ are a good foundation to be successful in information technology jobs.
