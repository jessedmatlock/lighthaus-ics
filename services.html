About › Our Services

ICS provides today’s companies with flexible workforce solutions.

We meet all candidates personally in order to understand their career objectives and to help them find roles that will best suit their talents, experience, and aspirations. Relying on ICS to manage your workforce frees you to focus your resources on what you do best: your company’s core business.

Contract Placement

Contractors are the preferred staffing solution when your business is undergoing change. ICS offers your business the flexibility to add staff in support of new initiatives and to remove personnel as projects wind down. Whether you need a single database development wizard or an entire project team, ICS delivers the talent you require for as long as you desire.

Temp To Perm Placement

Some companies prefer to evaluate a contractor’s on-the-job performance before extending an offer for full time employment. In these instances ICS is happy to accommodate your changing business needs by providing contract to hire staffing solutions. Once the decision is made, we will work with you on transitioning temporary staff into permanent positions.

Employee of Record Payroll Services

ICS provides Employer of Record services to help you manage your workforce of full-time and non-traditional employees, including contract workers, freelancers, employees, interns, retirees or alumni, and staff for special projects. Whether you need pass through payroll solutions for co-employment or just payroll for temporary workers our payroll solutions provide you with compliance tools for you and your staff.
