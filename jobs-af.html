Careers in Accounting

Accounting is one of those sectors that seem almost recession-proof. Businesses will always need an accounting department to review funds coming in and going out to survive financially and operate efficiently. Whether a business is thriving or has an off year, that business still needs numbers crunched, accounting statistics completed, and taxes filed each year.

As a growing leader in workforce solutions, ICS has the ability to help you use your accounting and auditing skills to begin climbing the business ladder with this reliable career choice. Careers in Finance

Whether you specialize in acquisitions and mergers, serve as an experienced CFO or enjoy ROI analysis, careers in finance are waiting for you. Jobs in finance are expected to rise at least 23 percent before 2020. With your education and experience in the finance career sector, you can play a role in creating or maintaining a corporation’s financial footing, powerhouse reputation and viable future.
